Science & Technology Museums

Visvesvaraya Industrial & Technological Museum

Interactive exhibits on engines and space tech named after engineering pioneer M. Visvesvaraya.

Nehru Science Centre, Mumbai

Features 500+ hands-on science exhibits, focusing on energy, sound, and technology innovations.

National Rail Museum, Delhi

Showcases over 100 locomotives and artifacts, chronicling the history of Indian railways.

Arts & Culture Museums

National Museum, New Delhi

Houses treasures from the Indus Valley Civilization, showcasing ancient art and artifacts.

Indian Museum, Kolkata

The oldest museum in India, featuring Mughal paintings and ancient sculptures dating back centuries.

Calico Museum of Textiles, Ahmedabad

Home to rare textiles from the 17th century, with exquisite embroidery and craftsmanship on display.

History and Heritage of India

India's rich history and diverse heritage are reflected in its numerous museums, showcasing artifacts, art, and culture from various eras.

Hampi, Karnataka

Once the capital of the Vijayanagara Empire, Hampi is renowned for its ancient Hindu temples, stone chariots, and stunning ruins set amidst a surreal landscape.

Konark Sun Temple, Odisha

This 13th-century temple, shaped like a colossal chariot, is dedicated to the Sun God Surya and is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Ellora Caves, Maharashtra

Ellora is famous for its monumental Hindu, Buddhist, and Jain rock-cut temples, including the magnificent Kailasa temple dedicated to Lord Shiva.

Religious Sites of India

India is home to a multitude of religious sites that reflect its diverse spiritual heritage, especially its Hindu temples and Sikh gurdwaras.

Kashi Vishwanath Temple, Varanasi

One of the most sacred Hindu temples dedicated to Lord Shiva, located on the banks of the Ganges in Varanasi.

Akshardham Temple, Delhi

A modern Hindu temple complex known for its grand architecture, spiritual exhibitions, and beautiful gardens.

Golden Temple, Amritsar

The holiest shrine of Sikhism, also known as Harmandir Sahib, renowned for its golden architecture and spiritual significance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What are the museum hours?

A: Most museums are open from 10 AM to 5 PM, Tuesday to Sunday. They are closed on Mondays.

Q: Are there any discounts available?

A: Yes, many museums offer discounts for students, seniors, and children under 5.

Q: Can I book tickets online?

A: Yes, tickets can be booked online through the respective museum's website.
